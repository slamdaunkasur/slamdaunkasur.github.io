$uicideboy$ - Paris

A SLAMDUNKASAUR PRODUCTION.

A lot of people dislike "new fans" but I'm not new to them and this video slaps. It's a banger in my opinion.

I love the way this video was edited and produced. If you don't know by now, the boy$ are a rap duo from New Orleans. This song goes way back to 2015 in their $ongsthatwewontgetsuedforbutattheendofthedaywereallgonnadieanyway$ era. That's how I first heard of them, when I was like 11 years old.

SLAMDUNKASAUR was their producing label that never really went anywhere. They made a new record label named G59 RECORDS. If you're new, it stands for GREYFIVENINE. They rap a lot about death and their grey color. 59 is "their" number so to speak. It comes from highway 59 that runs down south through New Orleans, Louisiana where they grew up.
